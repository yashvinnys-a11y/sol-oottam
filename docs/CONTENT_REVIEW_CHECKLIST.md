## Review criteria

For each item, verify:

1. the Tamil spelling is correct;
2. the `letters` array represents suitable learner-facing grapheme units and joins exactly to the target word;
3. the meaning and English gloss are accurate and age-appropriate;
4. the example sentence is grammatical and natural;
5. the sentence-completion prompt has one unambiguous answer;
6. all distractors are plausible but clearly incorrect in context;
7. accepted read-aloud variants are linguistically acceptable;
8. the hint and corrective feedback are clear for ages 7–12;
9. cultural references and icons are suitable;
10. difficulty is reasonable for the intended learner group.
